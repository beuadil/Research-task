> Referenced content — Ahrefs post featuring Sam Oh's AI/SEO predictions

**URL:** [https://www.linkedin.com/posts/ahrefs_4-ways-chatgpt-and-bard-will-change-seo-activity-7064298462695063552-Vd5u](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/ahrefs_4-ways-chatgpt-and-bard-will-change-seo-activity-7064298462695063552-Vd5u)

4 ways ChatGPT and Bard will change SEO

Sam Oh's predictions:

1. AI engines will further dominate queries that don't require expertise — simple definitions, basic listicles, simple how-to queries.
2. We'll see a decrease in wages for some SEO jobs. Many basic content writing tasks will be handled by AI operators. Subject matter experts are less likely to be as hard hit.
3. The bar for quality content will increase greatly. Google's helpful content update hints at how things are changing: content with expertise and depth of knowledge will be prioritized in the SERPs.
4. Video results will continue to attract more clicks. With text content sounding more generic, videos could help creators stand out.

SEO will not die — AI tools can't obtain unique, real-world experiences like humans do. SEO is multi-faceted; link building involves the very human aspect of building relationships.
