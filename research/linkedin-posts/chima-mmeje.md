## Post 5
> Direct post by Chima Mmeje

**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/chima-mmeje_my-top-seo-tips-for-2026-are-1-build-activity-7434271117927923713-miAH

My top SEO tips for 2026:

1. Build entity clusters that cover the full query fan-out — LLMs like Google's AI mode don't rely on a single search query. They run multiple related queries to build answers from a broader intent set. Topic clusters help you cover the full intent for the query while satisfying intent on a granular level.

2. Evolve reporting beyond traffic — clicks are declining. Track: LLM referrals, self-attribution, brand demand as an influence proxy, LLM citation depth, session quality signals.

3. Use community as a growth engine — for as long as humans exist, community will remain a growth lever: strengthening bonds with customers, understanding their language, collecting feedback, supporting product launches.

4. Optimize for Agentic AI and autonomous search — agentic browsing will be part of the future of search and conversion. Fix crawl frictions that would hurt conversion paths to ensure bots and agents can crawl your pages and take action.
