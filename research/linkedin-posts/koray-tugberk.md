## Post 3
> Referenced content — Bart Magera applying Koray's topical map framework to AI search

**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/bartmagera_ai-search-is-inevitable-i-like-to-start-activity-7373537406769274880-pX9C

AI search is inevitable. Looking at keywords alone is not enough — intent has changed dramatically with AI answers.

Question-mining stack (part of Koray Tugberk's topical map building framework):
- Google Search Console queries filtered by intent
- On-site search logs (zero-result terms first)
- SERP patterns: People Also Ask, related searches, snippets
- Community threads: Reddit, Slack, Stack Overflow
- Product docs feedback and roadmap objections

How to turn questions into pages:
- Answer block first (60-90 words in plain language)
- Entities explicit: canonical names, definitions, acronyms, synonyms
- Q&A sections: real questions as H2s, tight answers below
- Schema that matches reality: Article, HowTo, FAQ, plus Person and Organization

Off-site signals that feed AI models:
- Expert quotes in credible publications
- Small studies people will cite
- Podcasts, partner docs, ecosystem tutorials
- Consistent naming across all profiles
