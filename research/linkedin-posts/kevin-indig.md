## Post 2

**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/kevinindig_in-seo-we-obsess-over-where-we-rank-but-activity-7423732576139476992-sx3r

In SEO, we obsess over where we rank. But in the age of LLMs, the more critical question is who the AI thinks we are. I call this your AI Brand Archetype — the mental model the AI has constructed of your brand. Does it see you as a Tool? A Publisher? A Crisis? Or a Guru?

I analyzed Shopify using SE Ranking API data. I expected the AI to categorize Shopify as a "Tool" (platform, pricing, website builder). The data tells a completely different story:

~43% Motivation & Quotes ("inspirational quotes," "positive captions")
~22% Entrepreneurship Skills ("business ideas," "founder mindset")
...only ~14% Commercial Terms ("pricing," "plans")

The AI is 3x more likely to cite Shopify for lifestyle advice than for software pricing.

Strategic Takeaway: If you want to win in AI Search, you can't just target keywords. You need to think about how your organic ranks translate to how LLMs view your brand (AI Brand Archetype).

## Post 3

**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/kevinindig_want-an-8x-success-multiplier-on-your-ai-activity-7432767721030754306-iorn

Want an 8x success multiplier on your AI SEO strategy? It doesn't come from a new tool. It comes from how you manage the transition.

According to Prosci's benchmarking study, projects with excellent change management are six to eight times more likely to succeed.

The most successful growth leaders right now act as change agents using three principles:

1. Honesty over confidence — openly admit what we don't know yet (like the exact pipeline value of a ChatGPT mention), while acting on what we do know (that your brand missing from these answers is a risk).

2. Progress over perfection — don't try to overhaul the entire marketing machine at once. Build momentum in phases: Pilot → Expand → Embed.

3. Translation over broadcasting — stop reporting on outdated tactical metrics and start translating visibility into the language executives actually care about: Influence → Brand Recall → Pipeline.
