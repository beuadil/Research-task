## Overview

How a home services client was positioned to dominate Google AI Overviews, resulting in 370% increase in monthly AI referral traffic and visibility across 155 keywords in UK AI Overviews.

## Key Results

- Monthly AI referral traffic: +370%
- Client appeared in 155 AI Overview keywords (UK)
- Overall organic traffic: +82% (140k → 256k monthly sessions)
- Top-ranked keywords grew to 9,600+

## The Problem

Client had strong local rankings but was consistently outperformed by competitors in Google AI Overviews. Their Google Business Profile lacked complete information, and they had minimal presence on third-party review platforms that AI systems use for verification.

## The Three-Part Strategy

### 1. Google Business Profile Optimization
AI systems use GBP data as a primary structured data source — directly influencing whether a business appears in local packs and AI Overviews.

Actions taken:
- Completed all GBP fields with detailed business descriptions
- Maintained NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistency across all platforms
- Added high-quality images and videos
- Accumulated genuine customer reviews

### 2. Third-Party Platform Presence
AI models seek consensus across multiple trusted sources. Built review presence on Yelp, TripAdvisor, Trustpilot, and niche industry sites. Key: consistent business information everywhere.

### 3. Informational Query Content Strategy
Targeted "curiosity-driving" queries that encourage users to click beyond the AI Overview — comparisons, benefit-focused queries that require deeper exploration.

Content optimization approach:
- Lead with concise, extractable answers
- Use clear subheadings and structured formatting
- Provide unique insights beyond existing AI Overviews
- Create content that serves as the natural next step after the Overview

## Final Results

Blog achieved 1,498 monthly sessions from AI search (vs 804 six months prior). AI Overview visibility successfully converts to traffic when content provides additional value beyond the AI-generated summary.
